Siomar Ballan

I am a qualified counsellor and trainee psychotherapist, currently training at the Institute of Psychosynthesis, in London. I have been in private practice for 4 years.


I provide an empathic and non-judgemental environment where we can discuss and explore your concerns and feelings freely and safely. I offer an adaptable and creative approach that can be tailored to the individuals needs and unfolding process, honouring each persons individuality.


I have a background of over 8 years working in mental health, where I gained extensive experience of the complex suffering that can afflict the human spirit. I previously gained a BSc in psychology, in addition to having completed a variety of short courses in different approaches such as Gestalt, Jungian and person-centered counselling, providing me with a strong and diverse foundation of knowledge.




Alongside this, I also have a background as a yoga and meditation teacher. This informs my therapy practice in allowing me to incorporate techniques of mindfulness, assisting my clients in increasing self-awareness, their ability to stay in the ‘here and now’ and their sense of free will.


I am able to offer help and guidance with a range of issues including the following:


Anxiety and depression

Loss and Bereavement

Relationship difficulties or breakdown

Workplace stress and burnout

Anger and aggression

Trauma/ trauma resulting from abuse

I am professionally insured, UKCP registered and adhere to a strict code of ethics and principles for approved professional practice, receiving regular clinical supervision.
