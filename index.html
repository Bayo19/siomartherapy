A world of perpetual motion


Life appears to be increasing its momentum. Responsibilities and pressures can feel like a surging wave, continually increasing without a break, where we find little time to stop and reflect, to truly experience and explore what it means to be human and lead a fulfilling life.


At times this can result in life feeling overwhelming, meaningless or fragmented, giving rise to confusing emotional states such as depression, anxiety and burnout to name just a few.


Experiences of trauma, loss and bereavement, relationship issues or addiction can often augment this suffering and we may feel trapped and unable to cope with the varied challenges of life.


Working with a trustworthy professional can provide much needed help and guidance during such times of crisis and offer a confidential and warm space within which to explore our deeper selves. Therapy can support us in gaining a deeper sense of meaning and purpose in our life and a greater sense of contentment and self-awareness
